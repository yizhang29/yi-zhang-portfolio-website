More about me

Yi is a visionary designer shaping the next-level experience for private cloud at VMware by Broadcom. His exceptional work was recognized with a nomination and selection for the office of CTO awards . With a diverse background, Yi has contributed his design expertise to Poynt, United Nations, SevenRooms, and Mokuni games, crafting enterprise and customer products across various industries.

Beyond his professional endeavors, Yi is passionate about creating video games and animations. His thesis game project at the School of Visual Arts received official recognition, and his concept design was credited by ACM Siggraph . Two of his mobile games, 'Kitty in the Box 2'  and 'Albert & Otto' , were featured by app store. He is also an ACE certified personal trainer  who finds joy in workouts and challenges.

With a creative mind and a diverse skill set, Yi holds two product patents issued by CNIPA. These include the'Hornbill vacuum cleaner(CN303181579S)'  and 'Cartoon city view puzzle for kids(CN302918007S)' .
